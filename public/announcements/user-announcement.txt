🚀 SECTION 2: FIRST LOGIN - WHAT TO EXPECT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Go to the portal login page
STEP 2: Enter your email address in the "Email" field

FIRST-TIME PASSWORD CHANGE:
If your account has the "must change password" flag set:

🔑 SECTION 4: FORGOT YOUR PASSWORD?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If you forget your password or want to reset it:

STEP 1: Go to the login page
STEP 2: Click the "Forgot password?" link below the login form
STEP 3: Enter your email address
STEP 4: Click "Send Reset Link"
STEP 5: Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
STEP 6: Click the password reset link in the email
STEP 7: Enter your new password
STEP 8: Confirm your new password
STEP 9: Click "Reset Password"

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS:
• At least 8 characters long
• At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
• At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
• At least 1 number (0-9)

IMPORTANT: Password reset links expire after a limited time. If the link 
expires, you must request a new one.

🔐 SECTION 5: CHANGING YOUR PASSWORD
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To change your password at any time:

STEP 1: Look for the 🔑 (Key) icon in the header
STEP 2: Click the icon to open the Change Password page
STEP 3: Enter your current password
STEP 4: Enter your new password
STEP 5: Confirm your new password
STEP 6: Watch the checkmarks turn green as requirements are met

LIVE VALIDATION:
As you type your new password, you'll see checkmarks that indicate:
☐ → ✓ At least 8 characters
☐ → ✓ At least 1 uppercase letter
☐ → ✓ At least 1 lowercase letter
☐ → ✓ At least 1 number

All checkmarks must be green before you can save.

📝 SECTION 8: VIEWING AND ACKNOWLEDGING UPDATES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO VIEW UPDATES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Click "Updates" in the navigation
STEP 2: You'll see update cards on the main page
STEP 3: Each card shows:
        • Title of the update
        • Category badge
        • Posted date
        • Summary (first few lines)
        • Acknowledgement status
        • Deadline (if any)
STEP 4: Click on any card to read the full update

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO FILTER UPDATES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

At the top of the Updates page, you'll find:

TABS:
• ALL - Shows all updates
• By Category - Filter by specific categories

SEARCH:
• Type keywords to find specific updates
• Search looks at title and content

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO ACKNOWLEDGE UPDATES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Click on an update to open it
STEP 2: Read the full content carefully
STEP 3: Look for the "Acknowledge" button at the bottom
STEP 4: Click "Acknowledge" to confirm you've read it
STEP 5: A checkmark appears next to the update

IMPORTANT NOTES:
• Some updates have deadlines - acknowledge before the deadline
• Acknowledgement is tracked for compliance
• You cannot "un-acknowledge" an update
• Admins can see who has/hasn't acknowledged

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
UNDERSTANDING UPDATE STATUSES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🟢 ACKNOWLEDGED (Green checkmark)
   → You have read and acknowledged this update
   → No further action needed

🟡 NOT ACKNOWLEDGED (Yellow/Orange)
   → You have not yet acknowledged this update
   → Read it and click Acknowledge

⏰ HAS DEADLINE (Date badge)
   → This update requires acknowledgement by a specific date
   → Make sure to acknowledge before the deadline

📌 OBSOLETE
   → This update is no longer current
   → May have been replaced by a newer update

📅 SECTION 10: SUBMITTING OUTAGE/LEAVE REQUESTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW TO SUBMIT A REQUEST
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 1: Go to Outages → Outage Requests
STEP 2: Click "New Request" or "Submit Request" button
STEP 3: Fill in the required fields:
        • Start Date - When your outage begins
        • End Date - When your outage ends
        • Start Time - What time you'll be out
        • End Time - What time you'll return
        • Outage Reason - Select from dropdown:
          - Planned Time Off
          - Sick Leave
          - Emergency
          - Personal
          - Training
          - etc.
        • Remarks - Additional details (optional)
STEP 4: Attach any supporting documents (optional)
STEP 5: Review your request details
STEP 6: Click "Submit Request"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
UNDERSTANDING REQUEST STATUS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🟡 PENDING
   → Your request has been submitted
   → Awaiting approval from HR/Admin
   → You'll be notified of the decision

🟢 APPROVED
   → Your request has been approved
   → Will appear on the calendar as approved
   → You're confirmed for the outage dates

🔴 REJECTED
   → Your request was not approved
   → Check the rejection reason in remarks
   → You may submit a new request with changes

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VIEWING YOUR REQUEST HISTORY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

On the Outage Requests page:
• See all your submitted requests
• Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
• Click on any request to see details
• View approval/rejection remarks

📞 SECTION 18: NEED HELP?

If you have any questions or issues:

1. Click the ❓ Help button to re-open the demo guide

"I can't log in"

→ Check the rejection reason
→ Verify date availability on calendar
→ Submit a new request with adjustments
→ Contact HR for clarification
